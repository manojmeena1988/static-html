Lactosan Cheese Powder Blends

Our range of Cheese Powder Blends are based on combination of natural cheeses, melting salts and either whey powder, milk powders, maltodextrin and / or vegetable fat.

Cost-efficiency

When cost-efficiency and specific functionalities are your key drivers, we can help you replace an expensive ingredient with a Cheese Powder Blend. These versatile powders will enhance the taste and mouthfeel of your product and ensure a smooth flow in your dosing process.

Carefully prepared

Lactosan Cheese Powder Blends are carefully prepared with a combination of natural cheeses, melting salts and either whey powder, milk powders, maltodextrin and/or vegetable fat. The range includes vegetarian, halal, uncoloured and naturally coloured powders. Declarable as e.g. “Cheese powder, whey powder, salt”.
