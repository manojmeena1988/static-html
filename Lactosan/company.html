Lactosan through the years

To learn how it all started please take a short tour through our history line in the menubar to the left.

1942/1

In 1942 Lactosan was established by a small group of industrious Danish businessmen, including business man and designer, Mr. Tage Klint

1951

Did you know that we invented Cheese Powder? In 1951, Christian Jessen, factory manager at that time, experimented with the production of cheese powder made from melted cheese by using a spray drier. A customer had returned a consignment of processed cheese due to the lack of suitable storage conditions. Christian Jessen then started experimenting with the atomization of melted cheese into cheese powder for easier storage. This had never been done before, but after some time he developed a unique technique to produce cheese powder.
